Introduction

This website is aiming at connecting young Refugee talented to the outsides opportunities
We believe that in our community there are many young talented but they lack some of the comminucation and networking skills
and there are many coaches and organization that need talented youth but they lack information of where they can found those talented young African.
So based on this misconnecting between young talented and coach we think this website will have a huge importance in solving this challenge.
